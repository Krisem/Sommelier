## SAT-skjema

### Appearance
| Felt | Skala |
|---|---|
| **Intensity** | pale – medium – deep |
| **Colour, white** | lemon – gold – amber |
| **Colour, rosé** | pink – pink-orange – orange |
| **Colour, red** | purple – ruby – garnet – tawny |

### Nose
| Felt | Skala |
|---|---|
| **Intensity** | light – medium – pronounced |
| **Aroma characteristics** | primær / sekundær / tertiær (se lexicon under) |

### Palate
| Felt | Skala |
|---|---|
| **Sweetness** | dry – off-dry – medium – sweet |
| **Acidity** | low – medium – high |
| **Tannin** | low – medium – high |
| **Alcohol** | low – medium – high |
| **Body** | light – medium – full |
| **Flavour intensity** | light – medium – pronounced |
| **Flavour characteristics** | primær / sekundær / tertiær |
| **Finish** | short – medium – long |

### Conclusions
| Felt | Skala |
|---|---|
| **Quality** | poor – acceptable – good – very good – outstanding |

## Wine Lexicon (aromaer og smaker)

### Primary – druen og alkoholgjæringen
| Gruppe | Eksempler |
|---|---|
| **Floral** | blossom, rose, violet |
| **Green fruit** | apple, pear, gooseberry, grape |
| **Citrus fruit** | grapefruit, lemon, lime, orange |
| **Stone fruit** | peach, apricot, nectarine |
| **Tropical fruit** | banana, lychee, mango, melon, passion fruit, pineapple |
| **Red fruit** | redcurrant, cranberry, raspberry, strawberry, red cherry, red plum |
| **Black fruit** | blackcurrant, blackberry, blueberry, black cherry, black plum |
| **Herbaceous** | green bell pepper, grass, tomato leaf, asparagus |
| **Herbal** | eucalyptus, mint, fennel, dill, dried herbs |
| **Spice** | black/white pepper, liquorice |
| **Fruit ripeness** | unripe – ripe – dried – cooked |
| **Other** | wet stones, candy |

### Secondary – etterprosessering
| Kilde | Aromaer |
|---|---|
| **Yeast** (lees, autolysis, flor) | biscuit, pastry, bread, toasted bread, bread dough, cheese, yogurt |
| **Malolactic** conversion | butter, cream, cheese |
| **Oak** | vanilla, cloves, coconut, cedar, charred wood, smoke, chocolate, coffee |

### Tertiary – modning
| Type | Aromaer |
|---|---|
| **Red wine** | dried fruit, leather, earth, mushroom, meat, tobacco, wet leaves, forest floor, caramel |
| **White wine** | dried fruit, orange marmalade, petrol, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, almond, hazelnut, honey, caramel |
| **Deliberately oxidised** | almond, hazelnut, walnut, chocolate, coffee, caramel |

## Hvordan dette knytter til Polets klokker

| WSET-felt | Polet-klokke |
|---|---|
| Body | **Fylde** (1–12) |
| Acidity | **Friskhet** (1–12) |
| Tannin | **Garvestoffer** (1–12) |
| Sweetness | **Sødme** (1–12, kun hvit/musserende) |
| Flavour intensity | **Frukt** / **Krydder og urter** (klokker når relevant) |

Bruk WSET-vokabularet til presise smaksnotater, Polets klokker til kvantifiserbar sammenligning på Vinmonopolet.
